Privacy Policy for DailyList Pro

Effective Date: Aug 12, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Suiner ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the DailyList Pro mobile application (the "App").

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Our policy is simple: we do not collect any personal information or data from our users.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The DailyList Pro app is designed to be a completely private and offline tool. We want to be crystal clear about the data we DO NOT collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information like your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No User Content is Transmitted: The lists, titles, and descriptions you create in the app are stored exclusively on your local device. This data is never transmitted, uploaded, or saved to any external server or database. We have no access to it.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics tools to track your interaction with the app.

3. How Your Data is Stored and Used

All data you input into DailyList Pro (such as task titles, descriptions, and their completion status) is stored locally in the app's private storage on your own device (phone or tablet). This data is used only to provide the app's core functionality, which is to let you create, view, and manage your lists. When you mark a task as complete, that status is saved on your device so you can see your progress when you reopen the app. If you uninstall the app, this data is typically deleted from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that would collect data from you.

5. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages to use.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the app, please feel free to contact us. We are here to help.
