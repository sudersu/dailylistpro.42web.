Privacy Policy

Organize Your Day, Simply.

Welcome to DailyList Pro, a minimal and powerful to-do list application designed for simplicity and privacy. Create lists for your shop inventory, daily chores, or any important task. Mark items as complete and stay perfectly organized. All your data stays on your device, ensuring total privacy.

Core Features

Simple & Fast Interface

Quickly add titles and descriptions to your tasks. The clean and intuitive interface helps you focus on what matters most, without any distractions.

Easy Progress Tracking

Simply tap to mark any task as complete. A clear visual checkmark lets you see your progress at a glance whenever you reopen the app.

100% Private and Offline

Your privacy is our priority. Your lists are never sent to a server. Everything is stored securely and locally on your own device.
